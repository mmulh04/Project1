About Us


Through our rehoming and rehabilitation programs, we strive to ensure that each animal finds its perfect match and embarks on a journey towards a brighter future. Our team of dedicated professionals works tirelessly to assess the needs of each animal, providing them with the care and support they need to heal both physically and emotionally. But our mission extends beyond just finding homes for animals. We are committed to building a community of compassion and empathy, where every individual feels empowered to make a difference in the lives of our furry friends. That's why we have implemented a robust volunteering scheme, welcoming individuals from all walks of life to join us in our mission.


Volunteers play a crucial role in everything we do, from socializing with animals and assisting with daily care routines to helping with fundraising events and educational programs. Their passion and dedication not only enrich the lives of our animals but also strengthen the bond between humans and animals in our community.


Interested in our volunteering scheme? Click the button below and contact us and enter in the subject as 'volunteering'. We're always open for new volunteers.


Together, with your support, we can create FurEver Homes where every animal's story begins anew. Join us in our mission to make the world a better place for animals, one wagging tail and purring friend at a time.

Where to find us

Your destination for finding loving companionship with our adorable awaiting animals is nestled on the outskirts of Belfast, Northern Ireland.
You can locate us just a short drive away from the city centre, offering a peaceful retreat where you can connect with your future furry friend. Our cozy centre is easily accessible, providing a warm and welcoming environment for both our animals and visitors alike.
Come visit us and discover the perfect companion waiting to bring joy and warmth into you home.
